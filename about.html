Our Story

Roast & Ritual began as a neighborhood dream — a place where great beans meet simple rituals. We partner with ethical farms, roast in small batches, and train our baristas to treat every cup as a craft.

  • Sourcing: Direct relationships with farmers who share sustainable practices.
  • Roasting: Small batch roasts tailored to highlight each origin.
  • Community: Coffee that brings people together — from morning commuters to afternoon readers.
